## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**
A: Yes. Completely. No login, no credit card, no trial. The audit is free because surfacing your overspend is the point — it shows where Credex credits can help if you have significant savings.

**Q: How accurate is the math?**
A: Very. Unlike AI-powered tools, the audit engine uses deterministic, hardcoded rules against current pricing data (sourced directly from vendor pricing pages, updated weekly). Every savings figure is traceable to an arithmetic calculation you can verify yourself.

**Q: Does this store my company's data?**
A: The tool stores your audit results (tools, plans, savings figures) in a database to generate shareable links. It does not store the email you enter unless you explicitly choose to save your report. Identifying details (company name, email) are never shown in the public shared version.

**Q: What tools does it cover?**
A: Currently: Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Claude, ChatGPT, Anthropic API, OpenAI API, Gemini, and Windsurf. These cover the vast majority of AI developer tool spend at early-stage startups. We're adding more based on what users enter.

**Q: What happens if I enter my email?**
A: We email you a copy of your full audit report. If your savings are above $500/mo, a Credex team member may reach out to discuss how to capture those savings through AI infrastructure credits. You can opt out instantly. We don't spam.
